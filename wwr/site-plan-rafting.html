Overview

Purpose

Experience the joy that is white water rafting near the Salt Lake area. The purpose of this website is to advertise our company, making it easy and manageable for people interested to find more out about our company.

Audience

The target audience is outdoorsy people near our location. Q1: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the webiste? A1: How to book appointments, how to contact the company, our location address, phone number, email, etc. Q2: What are their needs that aren't being met? A2: Currently they cannot access the information about the company and book times to go rafting. Q3: How will my audience usually access my site? A3: All sorts of devices, including desktops, laptops, iPads, and phones.
